Fusion Light Hotspot

Basic Info

  • Appropriate location: Outdoor
  • Connectivity: WiFi, BLE, Ethernet
  • Introduction date: Q1 2022

Details about product

  • Based on Onion Omega 2S+ Computer module running Linux
  • RAK2287 mPCIE LPWAN Concentrator with Semtech SX1302 chip and ZOE-M8Q GPS
  • ECC608 for crypto operations
  • ESP32-C3 for BLE
  • Providing Wifi, BLE and Ethernet connectivity
  • with optional 4G Modem
  • Primarily intended for outdoor use
